Topic : Agricultural robotics

Agricultural robots are are the machines used to automate the process of agriculture. They are used for most agriculture activities which can be planting, harvesting, and recollecting data from the plants. It is also an important tool as they can measure more how to use a pesticide, and that way and using other techniques they improve the quality of lots of plants


The main technologies employed in this sector are :
Actuators - electric motors, hydraulic cylinders, pneumatic cylinders
Communication technologies - Wi-fi, cellular network, bluetooth
AI - machine learning, artificial narrow intelligence
